/copyini



The /copyini command is used to create a copy of a whole section in an INI file.

Synopsis

/copyini <inifile> [section] [newsection]

Switches

None

Parameters

<inifile> - The filename to copy from/to.
<section> - The section name to copy.
<newsection> - The name of the new copy.

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v7.84 (23 June 2026)
