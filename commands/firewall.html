/firewall



The /firewall command changes the firewall settings.

Note: This command has been renamed into /proxy in v7.0.

Synopsis

/firewall [on|off]
/firewall [-cmN[+|-]d] <server> <port> <userid> <password>
/firewall [-cmN[+|-]d] <server:port> <userid> <password>

Switches

-c - Clears the userid and password values.
-mN - Sets the connection type, where N is 4 or 5 for Socks4 or Socks5, or p for proxy.
+d - Enable sending DCC's though a firewall.
-d - Disables sending DCC's though a firewall.

Parameters

on - Enable the firewall.
off - Disable the firewall. <server> - The server.
<port> - The port.
<userid> - The username.
<password> - The password.

Example
