/ialfill



The /ialfill command fills the IAL by sending a /WHO #channel to the server and processing the WHO reply.

Synopsis

/ialfill <nick|channel>

Switches

-f - Force refill the IAL.

Parameters

<nick|channel> - The channel to fill

Example

/ialfill #fill

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v7.48 (15 Apr 2017)

Notes

None
