/perform



The /perform command enables or disables the perform section in the options (ALT + O > Connect). You can display its status without including a parameter.

Synopsis

/perform [on | off]

Switches

None

Parameters

on - Enable the raw event processing.
off - Disable the raw event processing.

Example

Display perform status: 
/perform
Enable perform: 
/perform on
Disable perform: 
/perform off

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v5.8 (14 Dec 2000)
