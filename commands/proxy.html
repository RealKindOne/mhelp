/proxy

/proxy



The /proxy command changes the firewall settings.

Synopsis

/proxy [on|off]
/proxy [-cmN[+|-]d] <server> <port> <userid> <password>
/proxy [-cmN[+|-]d] <server:port> <userid> <password>

Switches

-c - Clears the userid and password values.
-mN - Sets the connection type, where N is 4 or 5 for Socks4 or Socks5, or p for proxy.
+d - Enable sending DCC's though a firewall.
-d - Disables sending DCC's though a firewall.

Parameters

on - Enable the proxy.
off - Disable the proxy.
<server> - The server.
<port> - The port.
<userid> - The username.
<password> - The password.

Example

Display proxy status: 
/proxy
Enable proxy: 
/proxy on
Disable proxy: 
/proxy off

Compatibility
