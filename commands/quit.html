/quit

/quit



The /quit command disconnects you from IRC and will give the optional message as the reason for your departure.

Synopsis

/quit [msg]

Switches

None

Parameters

[msg] - The optional quit message

Example

/quit I was never here.

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v2.1a (28 Feb 1995)

Notes

None
