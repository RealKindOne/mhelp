/sockpause

/sockpause



The /sockpause command allows mIRC to pause/restart the reading from winsock. The packet are not lost, it's just that on sockread will stop triggering until you restart the reading.

Synopsis

/sockpause [-r] <name>

Switches

-r - Restarts the reading for the socket.

Parameters

<name> - The name of the socket to reference.

Example

Resume the incoming data reading for 'mySocket': 
/sockpause -r mySocket

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v5.3 (04 Jan 1998)

Notes

None
