/switchbar



The /switchbar command is used to enable or disable the mIRC switchbar. The switchbar is a toolbar with all the networks and channels on it displayed as buttons. You can display the switchbar status without including a parameter.

Synopsis

/switchbar <on|off>

Switches

None

Parameters

on - Enable the mIRC switchbar.
off - Disable the mIRC switchbar.

Example

Display switchbar status: 
/switchbar
Enable switchbar: 
/switchbar on
Disable switchbar: 
/switchbar off

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v6.32 (21 May 2008)
