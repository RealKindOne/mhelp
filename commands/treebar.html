/treebar



The /treebar command is used to enable or disable the mIRC treebar.

Synopsis

/treebar <on|off>

Switches

None

Parameters

on - Enable the treebar.
off - Disable the treebar.

Example

Enable the treebar: 
/treebar on
Disable the treebar: 
/treebar off
View the treebar status: 
/treebar

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v6.2 (23 Nov 2006)

Notes

The treebar cannot be disabled while in Compact mode.
