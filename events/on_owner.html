On Owner



The on OWNER event triggers whenever a user on a channel has been elevated to the level of channel owner. You can use $opnick to get the nickname being given owner status.

Synopsis

on <level>:OWNER:<#[,#]>:<commands>

Parameters

<level> - The corresponding access levels for the event to trigger.
<#[,#]> - The place, or places where the event listens, you can specify specific name of window, separate them by comma.
# - Any channel window.
#chan - Only in #chan window.
#chan,#chan2 - Only in #chan and #chan2 window.
<commands> - The commands to be performed when the event triggers.

Local Identifiers

$chan - The channel the nick is being ownered in.
$opnick - The nick being ownered.
$nick - The person who ownered the nick.

Example

Message the channel where the event occurs: 
on *:OWNER:#:msg # $opnick $+ : Congratulations on becoming an owner of # $+ !

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v5.61 (23 Sep 1999)
