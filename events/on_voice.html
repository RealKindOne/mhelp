On Voice



The on VOICE event triggers whenever a user on a channel has been elevated to voice.

Synopsis

on <level>:VOICE:<#[,#]>:<commands>

Parameters

<level> - The corresponding access levels for the event to trigger.
<#[,#]> - The place, or places where the event listens, you can specify specific name of window, separate them by comma.
# - Any channel window.
#chan - Only in #chan window.
#chan,#chan2 - Only in #chan and #chan2 window.
<commands> - The commands to be performed when the event triggers.

Local Identifiers

$chan - The channel the nick is being voiced in.
$vnick - The nick being voiced.
$nick - The person who voiced the nick.

Example

Acknowledge when a user has had their user level elevated to voice: 
on *:VOICE:#:msg # $vnick now has a voice, thanks to $nick $+ .

Compatibility
