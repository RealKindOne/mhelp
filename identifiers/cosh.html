$cosh



The $cosh identifier returns the hyperbolic cosine of an angle of N radians.

Synopsis

$cosh(<N>)[.deg]

Parameters


N - This is the angle in radians for which you wish to retrieve the hyperbolic cosine.

You can use the letter 'e' or 'd' as *10^N, 
$cosh(5e6) = $cosh($calc(5*10^6))

Properties


deg - Sees N as degrees in a 360 degree circle instead of radians.

Example

//echo -a $cosh(30).deg

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v7.33 (27 May 2014)
