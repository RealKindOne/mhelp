$dir



{{Deprecated feature|new=$sdir
}}'''$dir''' displays the select folder dialog and returns the selected folder.

Synopsis

$dir="title" <dir>

Parameters


title - The optional title of the dialog, the quote are optional if your title does not have space
<dir> - the directory/file to be opened, can be a wildcard

Properties

None

Example

//echo -a $dir='select dir' c:\

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v2.1a (28 Feb 1995)

Notes

None
