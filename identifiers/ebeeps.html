$ebeeps



The $ebeeps identifier returns either $true or $false depending on whether or not sounds are enabled in the options dialog.

Synopsis

$ebeeps

Parameters

None

Properties

None

Example

Echo whether or not sounds are enabled: 
//echo -a $ebeeps

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v6.3 (16 Aug 2007)

Notes

None
