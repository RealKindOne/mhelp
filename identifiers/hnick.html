$hnick

$hnick



The $hnick identifier returns the nickname of a person who was being helped/dehelped during an On_Help or an On_DeHelp.

Synopsis

$hnick

Paramaters

None

Properties

None

Example

A scripted event that will echo the nickname of the person who was just helped to the active window 
ON *:HELP:#:echo -a $hnick

Notes

None
