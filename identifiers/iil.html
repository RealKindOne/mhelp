$iil



The $iil identifier returns the Nth address in the internal invite list. You must perform "/mode #channel I" to fill the internal invite list.

Synopsis

$iil(#chan,N)[.property]

Parameters

#chan - The channel you want the invite from.
N - The Nth invite address in the channel, use N = 0 for the total number of addresses.

Properties

.by - Returns the nickname who set the invite.
.date - Returns the date when the invite was set.
.ctime - Returns the date when the invite was set, in $ctime format.

Example

Echo total numbers of invites for #mirc: 
//echo -a $iil(#mirc,0)

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v6.17 (17 Feb 2006)

v6.17: Added .by, .ctime, and .date properties.
