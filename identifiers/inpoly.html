$inpoly



The $inpoly identifier returns $true if the specified point is inside the specified polygon, $false otherwise.

Synopsis

$inpoly(x,y,x1,y1,x2,y2,x3,y3,...)

Parameters

x - The x coordinate of the point.
y - The y coordinate of the point.
x1 - The x coordinate of the first point.
y1 - The y coordinate of the first point.
x2 - The x coordinate of the second point.
y2 - The y coordinate of the second point.
xN - The x coordinate of the Nth point.
yN - The y coordinate of the Nth point.

Properties

None

Example

None
