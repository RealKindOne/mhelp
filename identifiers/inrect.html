$inrect



The $inrect identifier returns $true if the specified point is inside the specified rectangle, $false otherwise.

Synopsis

$inrect(x1,y1,x,y,w,h)

Parameters

x1 - The x coordinate of the point.
y1 - The y coordinate of the point.
x - The x coordinate of the rectangle.
y - The y coordinate of the rectangle.
w - The width of the rectangle.
h - The height of the rectangle.

Properties

None

Example

//echo -a $inrect(50,50,10,10,100,100)

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v5.3 (13 Dec 1997)

Notes

None
