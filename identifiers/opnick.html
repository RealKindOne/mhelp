$opnick



The $opnick identifier returns the nickname of a person who was opped/deopped during an On_Op, On_DeOp, On_Owner, and On_DeOwner event.

Synopsis

$opnick

Parameters

None

Properties

None

Example

A scripted event that will echo the nickname of the person who was just opped to the active window: 
ON *:OP:#:echo -a $opnick

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v4.5 (30 Jun 1996)

Notes

None
