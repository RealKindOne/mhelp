$tanh



The $tanh identifier returns the hyperbolic tangent of an angle of N radians

Synopsis

$tanh(<N>)[.deg]

Parameters

N - This is the angle in radians for which you wish to retrieve the hyperbolic tangent.

Properties

.deg - Sees N as degrees in a 360 degree circle instead of radians.

Example

//echo -a $tanh(1).deg

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v7.33 (27 May 2014)
