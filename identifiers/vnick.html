$vnick



The $vnick identifier returns the nickname of a person who was voiced/devoiced during an On_Voice or an On_DeVoice event.

Synopsis

$vnick

Parameters

None

Properties

None

Example

A scripted event that will echo the nickname of the person who was just voiced to the active window: 
on *:VOICE:#:echo -a $vnick

Compatibility

Added: mIRC v4.72 (09 Jan 1997)

Notes

None
